Mapo Tofu

Description

This Japanese-style Mapo Tofu dish is a staple in my weekly rotation of dishes since it can be cooked in under 30 minutes.

The sauce has a gravy-like texture and is savory and aromatic. It provides a good, hearty meal when eaten with rice.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • Tofu (soft)
  • Ground turkey or ground pork
  • Water
  • Corn starch
  • Oyster sauce
  • Green onions
  • Sriracha (optional)

Steps

  1. Mince 4 to 5 cloves of garlic (the more the merrier)
  2. Cube the tofu
  3. Make sauce by mixing water, corn starch and oyster sauce (For each pound of meat use: 3 tbsp oyster sauce, 1.5 tbsp corn starch, 1 cup of water)
  4. Heat garlic in oil until for a few minutes
  5. Add meat and cook until its just about ready
  6. Add your prepared sauce and cook until the cloudiness disappears
  7. Add tofu (Be gentle to not break the tofu while stirring)
  8. Add a little sriracha to bring out flavor; add more if you want more kick
  9. Turn off heat and add green onions
